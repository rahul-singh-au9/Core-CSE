8) Differentiate between SQL and NO-SQL database?-

1.SQL pronounced as "S-Q-L" or as "See-Quel" is primarily called RDBMS or Relational Databases whereas NoSQL is a Non-relational or Distributed Database.

2.SQL databases are table based databases whereas NoSQL databases can be document based, key-value pairs, graph databases.

3.SQL databases are vertically scalable while NoSQL databases are horizontally scalable.

4.SQL databases have a predefined schema whereas NoSQL databases use dynamic schema for unstructured data.

5.SQL requires specialized DB hardware for better performance while NoSQL uses commodity hardware
